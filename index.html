Liquidation Preference
What do the variants mean?
None (pro-rata)Everyone receives exactly their ownership % of exit proceeds. No priority, no waterfall — fairest distribution.
1× non-participatingInvestors first get their capital back, then forfeit their pro-rata share of the remainder. Today's standard for reputable VCs.
1× participatingInvestors get their capital back AND keep their full share of the remainder. "Double dip" — unfavorable for founders at mid-range exits.
2× non-participatingInvestors receive 2x their capital before founders see anything. Occurs in down-rounds or tough negotiations.
